Our Research Journey: Problem to Objectives

Understand the core challenge we're addressing and the specific goals guiding our innovative AI solution for the fashion industry.

The Research Problem

How can machine learning be utilized to revolutionize the fashion industry by differentiating between old and new fashion styles, enhancing sustainability, and understanding consumer psychology?

↓

Main Objective

Leveraging Machine Learning for Classification and Differentiation of Old Fashion and New Fashion Clothing in the Textile Industry.

↓

Specific Objectives

  • Old Style and New Style Similarity Measuring
  • Reducing Similarity with Suggestions
  • Psychological Impact of Clothing Styles
  • Sustainability and Environmental Impact

Core Features

  • → AI Trend Recognition: Deep learning to classify fashion by era and pattern
  • → Sustainability Analyzer: Predicts and scores eco-impact of designs
  • → Style Recommender: Helps reduce old-new style similarities
  • → Consumer Psychology Engine: Forecasts impact of style on mood, confidence

Our Methodology in Action

Our methodology focuses on leveraging advanced machine learning techniques to achieve accurate fashion trend analysis and prediction. The process involves several key stages, each designed to ensure comprehensive and insightful results:

Data Collection and Preprocessing

Gathering a diverse dataset of activewear fashion images and associated metadata, including cleaning, normalizing, and augmenting data to ensure quality and variety for model training. The dataset is similar to MAS Holdings datasets.

Feature Extraction

Utilizing deep learning models, such as Convolutional Neural Networks (CNNs), to extract relevant features from fashion images. These features capture stylistic elements, patterns, and silhouettes.

Trend Classification and Prediction

Implementing classification algorithms to categorize fashion items by era and pattern, and employing time-series analysis for forecasting future trends.

Similarity Measurement

Developing algorithms to quantify similarities between old and new styles, enabling identification of unique trends and suggestions to reduce similarities.

Sustainability Modeling

Integrating modules to predict and score the environmental impact of designs based on materials, production processes, and lifecycle analysis.

Psychological Impact Analysis

Building models to forecast the psychological impact of clothing styles on mood and confidence, providing insights for designers.

Model Training and Evaluation

Iteratively training and evaluating machine learning models using various metrics to ensure high accuracy, robustness, and generalization capabilities.

This comprehensive approach ensures that LULULEMON Activewear provides a robust and intelligent platform for sustainable activewear innovation. The outcome supports innovative, eco-friendly fashion designs.
